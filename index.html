Happy Raksha Bandhan! Yeh sirf ek dhaga nahi, hamare pyaar, saath aur yaadon ka prateek hai. Tumhara saath hamesha meri zindagi ka ek anmol hissa raha hai. Har muskil waqt mein, jab bhi mujhe zarurat thi, tumne apni puri taqat se mera saath diya, meri har pareshani mein mere saath khadi rahi. Tumhare saath guzaari har ek pal mere liye khas hai. Tumhara pyaar aur support meri zindagi ko roshni deta hai. Tumse kuch seekhne ko har din milta hai, aur tumhara saath meri duniya ko aur bhi khoobsurat banaata hai. Bhagwan tumhe apni saari khushiyan de aur tumhari har dua ko poora kare. Tum hamesha khush raho, safal raho, aur apni zindagi mein sab kuch pao jo tum chahti ho. Happy Raksha Bandhan, meri pyaari behna!
